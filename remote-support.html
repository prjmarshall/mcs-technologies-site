Using TeamViewer for Remote Support

1

Download TeamViewer

Click the link above or download here for the Quick Support version. It's free and lightweight.

2

Run the Application

Open the TeamViewer installer and run the Quick Support version. No installation required - it runs directly.

3

Share Your ID

Your unique ID appears immediately. Share this code with us via phone or email to establish a connection.

4

Approve Access

When we request access, you'll see a prompt. Click "Allow" to let us view and control your screen.

5

Session Active

We'll work on your issue while you watch. You can pause or end the session at any time.

✓

Session Complete

After we finish, simply close TeamViewer. The connection is automatically terminated and cannot be reused.
